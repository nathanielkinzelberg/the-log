## Journal

Block 6, Week 2, Day 2 — cluster deadlifts and tempo bench, and the body didn't show up the way Sunday's session suggested it would. The deadlifts climbed to 195 kg for the final cluster set, which is a load higher than Week 1's ceiling, but the RPE arc told a harder story: 6.5 on the opener, 7 on the second, then 8 on the third — the bar felt heavier than the numbers deserved, and every set came with feedback that something was off. Tempo bench at 125, 127.5, and 130 kg followed the same pattern — the first set was clean and controlled, the second started to bite, and by 130 kg the last rep was fully maximal. The accessories wrapped it all up in the worst way: the glutes cramped hard on the hip thrusts by set 2 and the lat pulldowns jumped from RPE 7 to 9.5 on the same weight before dropping to 92.3 kg for the final set. Some Days are just bad — the loads went up, the program did its job, and the body said it felt every kilogram of it.
