Overall a very light day, exactly as programmed.

Protein stayed on point even with the low mechanical output — eggs and cottage cheese first thing, chicken thighs with rice post-session, Greek yogurt before bed. Around 200 g for the day; muscle protein synthesis doesn't take deload days off.
