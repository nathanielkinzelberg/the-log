## Journal

Week 5 Recovery Deload kicks off today, and walking into the gym with "no stress" as the explicit brief was a genuinely unusual feeling. Squats sat at 152.5 kg for two sets of five — both came in at RPE 6, both felt like they could have been warmups. Bench followed the same pattern: 132.5 and 135 for sets of five at RPE 5.5, easy enough that I barely broke a sweat, though the lighter load made it harder to find a good setup position. Accessories were relaxed — machine chest press at 160, leg extensions at the stack, and French press at 50 kg to close it out. Very chill day overall, exactly what the body needs after closing Block 4 with a 225 kg deadlift single.

Protein still got handled properly on a deload day — cottage cheese and eggs in the morning, chicken and rice post-session, Greek yogurt in the evening. Roughly 210 g across the day; the deload doesn't reduce the anabolic need, just the mechanical stimulus.

😴🏋️ Deload mode: activated. The barbell thinks it's heavy; the body knows better.
