## Journal

Recovery Deload Day 4 closed out Week 5 exactly as the program intended — light, controlled, and done with energy still in the tank. The deadlifts at 170 kg were genuinely comfortable; both sets of five landed at RPE 5, which for a 200+ kg puller is practically a warmup, but moving well on deload days is its own kind of discipline. Lat rows at 180 kg for two sets of six felt heavier than the weight on the bar warranted, though the second set actually tightened up and finished at RPE 6 with a clean back — that's the signal I was looking for. Hamstring curls at 99 were steady through the first set and crept up to RPE 9 on the second, a small reminder that the posterior chain doesn't fully read the deload memo. One set of alternating DB curls at 18 kg closed things out at RPE 8, clean eight reps, nothing more to prove. Week 5 done — the system is ready to reload.
